System Introduction

System purpose

Distribuidora Los Amigos is a comprehensive management platform designed to administer customers, suppliers, products, inventory, orders, and reports for a poultry distribution company. It centralizes information and allows operations with permissions differentiated by role.

The system is organized into independent modules that share common services for security, logging, integrity auditing (DVH/DVV), and internationalization.

Suggested workflow

  1. Security: Create users, permissions, and families from the Management module.
  2. Masters: Load suppliers, products, and opening stock.
  3. Customers: Register customers and keep their information up to date.
  4. Orders: Generate orders, assign products, and monitor their status.
  5. Reports: Review stock alerts and sales analysis.
  6. Backup: Create and restore backups on a regular basis.

Roles and permissions

The system implements a security model based on permissions (patents) and families (roles). Families group permissions together and are assigned to users from the Management ‚Üí Assign role module.

Suggested role Typical permissions Responsibilities
Administrator Full management of users, security, and backups. Configure permissions, create families, and keep audits updated.
Sales Customers, orders, and commercial reports. Register new customers and manage daily orders.
Warehouse Inventory and products. Update stock levels and monitor expirations and availability.

Best practices and validations

  • Forms validate required fields, email formats, and duplicates.
  • Listings display information in grids with filters and sorting.
  • Critical operations request confirmation before execution.
  • All relevant actions are recorded in the log.

Tips for new users

Configure users and roles first to ensure each collaborator only sees the required modules.
Use contextual help with F1 on each screen to check specific procedures.
