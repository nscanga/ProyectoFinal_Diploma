Adjust stock quantities

Description

Allows you to update the available quantity of a product and optionally its stock type (unit of measure). Use it to correct inventory differences or record manual adjustments.

How to access

  1. From Stock ‚Üí Show stock select the product to adjust.
  2. Press Edit stock to open this form.

Editable fields

Field Description Required
Quantity New available quantity. It must be an integer greater than or equal to zero. Yes
Stock type Associated unit of measure (Unit, Box, etc.). Yes

Step by step

  1. Enter the new quantity in the numeric control.
  2. Select the corresponding unit of measure.
  3. Press Save. The system validates that the quantity is not negative and updates the stock via StockService.ModificarStock.
  4. The message ‚ÄúStock updated successfully‚Äù appears and the form closes.

Validations

  • Negative quantities are not allowed.
  • The stock type cannot be left blank.
  • Success and error messages are translated according to the active language.

Tips

Use positive adjustments for incoming goods and negative adjustments to record shrinkage.
After saving, refresh the stock view to display the new balance.
