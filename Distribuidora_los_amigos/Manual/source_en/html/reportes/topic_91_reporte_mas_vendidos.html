Top Selling Products

Introduction

This report shows the products with the highest sales volume for the selected period. It consolidates confirmed orders so you can quickly identify consumption trends.

It is ideal for planning purchases, defining commercial campaigns, or analyzing product profitability. You can easily adjust the time range and the number of items displayed in the ranking.

How to Access

To open this report:

  1. From the main menu, go to Reports.
  2. Select Top Sellers.
  3. The Top Selling Products window opens with default filters.

Procedure

1 Select the period

Choose dates in From and To and click Filter to update the data.

2 Adjust the ranking

Enter how many products you want to see in Top Products and click Update.

3 Analyze the results

Sort or filter the grid by category or amount to identify commercial opportunities and demand patterns.

4 Export the ranking

Click Export to Excel to generate a CSV and share it with purchasing, marketing, or management.

System validations

The system enforces the following rules:

  • The From date must be earlier than or equal to the To date; otherwise a warning is displayed.
  • The value for Top Products must be a positive integer greater than zero.
  • CSV export is disabled when the grid has no data.

Tips and recommendations

Combine this report with stock analysis to guarantee availability for the most demanded products.
Save different periods (monthly, quarterly) to compare trends and adjust prices or promotions.
